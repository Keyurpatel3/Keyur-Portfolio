Heat Exchanger Design

2024
Thermal Analysis Fluid Dynamics CAD Design

Project Overview

This project involved the design and optimization of an industrial heat exchanger system for a manufacturing facility. The primary goal was to improve thermal efficiency while maintaining cost-effectiveness and ensuring reliable operation.

The heat exchanger was designed to handle high-temperature fluid transfer between two process streams, with a focus on minimizing energy losses and maximizing heat transfer efficiency.

Technical Details

  • Heat transfer area: 250 m²
  • Operating temperature range: 150°C - 300°C
  • Flow rate: 500 m³/h
  • Pressure rating: 10 bar
  • Material: Stainless Steel 316L

Results & Impact

The redesigned heat exchanger achieved a 25% improvement in thermal efficiency compared to the previous system. This resulted in significant energy savings and reduced operational costs for the facility.

Key improvements included:

  • Enhanced heat transfer coefficient through optimized fin design
  • Reduced pressure drop across the system
  • Improved maintenance accessibility
  • Extended service life through better material selection
